About Me

I am currently studying Software Engineering at Mcmaster University. As a young engineer driven to learn, I spend alot of my time looking for and tinkering with different technologies. Infact a majority of what I have learned so far comes from creating projects on my free time. In each of my projects I try to pick a new tech and then combine it with something i've done in the past, this way I have the oppurtunity to improve on what I already know while also learning something new.

Besides creating projects, I am also an avid competitive programmer; representing McMaster University at the ICPC. These competitions are alot of fun and I often get the chance to solve some really intresting problems.
