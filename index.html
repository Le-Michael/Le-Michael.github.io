Kinundrum

What is it?

Kinundrum is an educational web application designed by some very talented designers at the University of Toronto. It's goal is to help undergraduate students study anatomy in an intuitive way by presenting them with a variety of case studies and follow up exercises to match each scenario.

Why am I working on this?

I often find myself spending too much time worrying about the aesthetics of my projects. Since all the designs were already done, it was the perfect opportunity for me to spend more time working with and learning more about backend and lower level frontend development.
